The Narwhal

The male Narwhal has a helical tusk that is actually a protruding upper-left canine.

  • Scientific Name: Monodon Monoceros
  • Average Length: 13 - 18ft
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 50 years
  • Film Cameo: Elf

Narwhal live year round in the Artic Sea near Greenland. Their name derives from the Old Norse 'nár' meaning 'corpse' in reference to their greyish skin, which would remind sailors of a bloated corpse.
